## Essence

**To:** letters@essence.com
**Subject:** A Stock Market Game Where Black Culture IS the Economy

Hi,

For every $100 in wealth held by White families, Black families hold $15. Only 18% of Black households own any stocks. The tools that exist to teach investing , Investopedia simulators, school worksheets , weren't designed for us. They trade Apple and Tesla with Bloomberg news and assume a familiarity with Wall Street that millions of Black Americans don't have.

I built something different. BLK Exchange is a stock market simulator where you trade 36 fictional companies that represent the Black economy: a natural hair care brand, a streetwear label, a Black-owned streaming platform, a community bank. Real cultural news moves the market. AI coaches you through investing concepts as you play. You learn diversification, risk, and portfolio strategy not from a lecture, but from trades you're actually making with companies you actually care about.

The app is grounded in Culturally Responsive Education research. NYU's Metropolitan Center found that Black students who received culturally relevant teaching had better academic outcomes and stronger identity development. I wrote a full research paper with 28 citations backing why this works.

I'm not a developer. I'm the Director of Strategy and Innovation at Radio Milwaukee and a Howard University grad. I built this in a week using AI coding tools because I couldn't find the financial literacy tool I wanted for my own community.

It's free, it's live, and it's in beta: https://blkexchange.com

Full press release attached. Would love to share more.

Tarik Moody
https://buymeacoffee.com/tarikmoody (supporting the project)

## Notes

- **Personalize every email.** If you can reference a specific recent article the editor wrote, do it. That's the difference between getting read and getting deleted.
- **Send Tuesday through Thursday, 9-11am ET.** That's when editors are triaging pitches.
- **Follow up exactly once** after 5-7 business days. Keep it short: "Following up on the BLK Exchange pitch below. Happy to chat if it's a fit."
- **Don't send all of these the same day.** Stagger across a week so you can adjust your pitch based on early responses.
