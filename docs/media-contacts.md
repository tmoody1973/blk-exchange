## Pitch strategy

1. **Don't blast the press release.** Personalize each email with 2-3 sentences about why this story fits THEIR outlet specifically.
2. **Subject line matters.** Try: "Howard Grad Builds AI Stock Market Game for Black Financial Literacy — Without Writing Code"
3. **Lead with the stat.** "$15 for every $100" or "Only 8 Black-owned companies are publicly traded" — those stop editors mid-scroll.
4. **Attach the press release as a doc** and paste the first 3 paragraphs in the email body. Don't make them open an attachment to decide if they care.
5. **Include the live link.** https://blkexchange.com — let them play it. The product sells itself.
6. **Follow up once** after 5-7 days if no response. Then move on.
